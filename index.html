Serving Howard County Since 2010

All American Heating & Cooling, Inc. has been repairing and installing ACs, heat pumps, furnaces and ducts in Howard County, MD and surrounding areas for over 35 years. Founded with a simple mission to provide reliable HVAC services with integrity and professionalism, we've grown to become Howard County's trusted HVAC provider.

In 2021, our residential operations rebranded as Greenfox Cooling, Heating and Plumbing and relocated to Columbia, MD under the same senior management. All American Mechanical continues to service existing commercial clients from our Ellicott City location. Our team of certified technicians brings decades of experience to every job, whether it's a simple repair or complete system installation.
